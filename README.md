# Slime Defense: The Great Journey

Welcome to Slime Defense. This is a 2D platformer adventure built over 45+ hours for Hack Club: The Game.

## The Adventure
Travel through vast lands and explore mysterious environments as you go on a quest to rid the world of the slime plague. Your journey requires platforming skills to navigate difficult terrain and survive.

## How to Play
* Movement: Use the Arrow Keys to run and explore the world.
* Combat: There are no weapons in this game. You must jump on a slime's head to defeat them.
* Bosses: Test your skills against powerful Slime Bosses at the end of your journey. Timing is everything.
